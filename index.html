The Six Guardians

These mathematical constants form the foundational pillars of the spectral analysis framework.

phi
The Golden Guardian
1.6180339887...
Role: Defines NP-class resonance alpha_NP = phi + 1/4
The golden ratio emerges in recursive structures - precisely what NP-complete problems exhibit through certificate branching. IBM Quantum tests confirm NP problems cluster at coherence values proportional to phi.
sqrt(2)
The Polynomial Guardian
1.4142135623...
Role: Defines P-class resonance alpha_P = sqrt(2)
Represents simplest algebraic irrationality - one constructible with finite operations. P-class problems follow single deterministic paths, never requiring the infinite recursion that phi embodies.
pi
The Circle Guardian
3.1415926535...
Role: Scales eigenvalue formula lambda_0 = pi/(10*alpha)
Pi bridges quantum Hamiltonians - wave functions oscillate in circular harmonics. Ground state energy depends on pi as the fundamental period of oscillation.
alpha
The Resonance Guardian
alpha_P=1.414 | alpha_NP=1.868
Role: Encodes complexity class structure in Hamiltonians
These are the "fingerprints" of complexity classes. Alpha determines how the Hamiltonian operator encodes problem structure - the resonance frequency of the computational quantum state.
CH2
The Coherence Guardian
Threshold: 0.95398265359
Role: Boundary separating P from NP in coherence space
Measures how "crystallized" a problem's solution structure is. P-class: CH2 ~ 0.95. NP-complete: CH2 ~ 0.9954. The gap is statistically significant (p < 0.001).
D3
The Fractal Guardian
D3(n) = Sum of base-3 digits
Role: Provides oracle-independent structure
The key to bypassing relativization. D3 is defined purely in terms of base-3 representation - no reference to computation. D3(n) is the same with or without an oracle, making spectral gaps derived from it oracle-independent.
